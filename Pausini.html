Laura Pausini

Laura Pausini is an Italian singer, producer, songwriter and television personality, winner of multiple international awards. Her career is distinguished by her singing in Italian and Spanish, but she has also recorded songs in Portuguese, English, French, and Catalan.

Her lyrics are mainly about love in its different forms and occasionally about social problems such as recisism, war or homophobia. He rose to fame in 1993, winning the newcomers section of the 43rd San Remo Music Festival with his debut single "La solitudine", which quickly became an anthem in Italy and a hit internationally, reaching number one in the Netherlands and Belgium. Her eponymous debut album was released in Italy in 1993 and became an international hit, selling more than 3 million copies worldwide.
